🤖 Free AI with Ollama

VendorForge runs entirely on Ollama — free, open-source AI that runs locally on your Mac. No API keys, no monthly bills, no data sent to the cloud. Your vendor data stays 100% on your machine.

STEP 01
Install Ollama
Download from ollama.com — it's a simple Mac app install, takes 2 minutes.
ollama.com/download
STEP 02
Pull a model
Open Terminal and run this command to download the AI model.
ollama pull llama3.2
STEP 03
That's it!
Ollama runs in the background. The green dot in the top-right confirms it's connected.
ollama serve
